FAQ

How do I upload a certificate?
To upload a certificate, log in to your account, go to your dashboard, and click on the "Upload Certificate" button. Fill in the required details and upload the document for verification.
How are activity points calculated?
Activity points are automatically calculated based on the type (Intracollege, Intercollege, State, National) and level of certificates uploaded. Each type has a predefined point value.
Can I edit a certificate after uploading it?
No, once a certificate is uploaded, it cannot be edited. Ensure all details are accurate before submission. Teachers may provide feedback for improvements.
What happens if I don't reach 100 activity points in four years?
If a student fails to reach 100 activity points within four years, they will be in a warning state after three years. It is crucial to collect the remaining points to reach the required total.
Can I change my user role from student to teacher or vice versa?
No, the user role (student or teacher) is assigned during signup and cannot be changed. If there are specific changes needed, please contact support.
How often should I check for notifications?
It is recommended to check for notifications regularly, especially if you have pending approvals or important updates. Notifications will be displayed on your dashboard.
